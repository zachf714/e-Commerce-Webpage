Examples of Things to do in Charlotte

  • Go to the Charlotte Motor Speedway
  • Go to the NASCAR Hall of Fame
  • Go to a Hornets Game
  • Go to a Panthers Game
  • Go to Carowinds
  • See some live music at the PNC Music Pavillion or NC Music Factory
  • Go to South Park Mall

Venues and Attractions

Charlotte is full of Attractions and Venues to choose from during a stay, or even just a day of fun. There is the simple mall trip at Concord Mills or South Park malls in which you can spend hours of your day, or you can spend your day having fun at an Ammusement park like Carowinds riding rollercoasters. There are also sports arenas like the TimeWarner Cable Arena where the Charlotte Hornets Basketball team plays and the Bank of America Stadium where the Carolina Panthers Football team plays.
