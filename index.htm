Charlotte and the Area

Charlotte is an area full of energy. Being the largest city in the state of North Carolina and the second largest state in the South East it should be full of energy. The area has a large night life with over 210 venues in the area ranging from clubs to live music venues. The area also is based on NASCAR having around 75% of the NASCAR teams based in the Charlotte area, and the NASCAR Hall of Fame in Charlotte.
